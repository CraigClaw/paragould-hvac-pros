# Paragould HVAC Pros Lead Buyer Prospect Plan

## Opportunity

HVAC has urgent seasonal demand and high-ticket replacement jobs; a focused city site can segment repair, replacement, and maintenance leads.

## Search Queries Reviewed

- Paragould AR HVAC contractors
- AC repair Paragould AR

## Best Buyer Profile

- Local business serving Paragould and Greene County.
- Weak website or no city/service page coverage.
- Answers calls quickly.
- Wants ac repair, hvac replacement, furnace repair, heat pump service leads.
- Will accept tracked calls/forms and give feedback on lead quality.

## Outreach Script

> Hi, is this the right person for new HVAC service jobs? I built a Paragould-focused quote request website for ac repair, hvac replacement, furnace repair, heat pump service. I am looking for one local company to send the leads to first. Would you be open to taking a few tracked leads as a trial and only paying if the quality makes sense?

## Trial Offer

- 7 days or first 5 qualified leads.
- Qualified lead: call over 60 seconds or form with name, phone, city, project type, and details.
- Start with pay-per-lead by job type, then move to exclusive monthly rental after proof.
